Frame: F450
Flight controller: DIY Arduino + MPU6050
Arduino: R3 clone
MPU6050: ITG/MPU
ESC: Simonk 30A *4
Motors: Generic A2212/13T 1000kv *4
Propellers: Generic 8045 & 1045
Battery: Bonka 2200MAh, 3s 35c, 70c burst, 11.1V 
Receiver: Flysky FS-IA6B
Transmitter: Flysky FS-i6
Power distribution: On frame PDB
Wiring overview: Jumper wires and XT60 for battery


###### Before the first flight:

1. Upload the Setup sketch.
2. Upload the ESC Calibration sketch.
3. Calibrate all four ESCs.
4. Upload the desired Flight Controller firmware.
